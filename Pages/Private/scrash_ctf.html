Scrash's Capture the Flag:

Game Mode Description:

  • Classic: Old Mode where you take the enemy flag and take it to your base to score you a point, but remember your team's flag has to be in your base;
  • Modern: Similar to the classic mode, only difference is that to retrieve the flag you have to take it to your base and not just touch it;
  • Steal Bacon: Contains only one flag in this mode, steal it and take it to your base to make it a point;
  • Free for All: The objective is to catch the flags that are spawn in random positions across the map and take this flag to the base with the corresponding color;
  • Three flags: Similar to the Free for All mode, but is in team;
  • One Base: One Flag and One Base, the base and the flag appeared in random places, take the flag and take it to that base;
  • Random Bases: Gameplay similar to free for all, but the bases appear in random places;

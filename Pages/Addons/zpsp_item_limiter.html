[ZPSp] Addon: Item Limiter

Description:

This addon allows limiting the number of extra itens buyed by round/map in zpsp_extraitems.ini file

Requeriments:

  • Amx 1.9 or higher
  • Zombie Plague Special 4.5 or higher

How to use:

When plugin enables, will be add a limit line in zpsp_extraitems.ini and you can change limit by round and by map (Similar with zclass limiter)
